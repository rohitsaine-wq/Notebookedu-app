Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Notebook respects the privacy of its users.

This policy explains how information may be collected and used when using the Notebook app.

1.Information We May Collect

The app may collect limited information such as:

  • Name or email address (if login is used)
  • Basic device or browser information
  • Basic usage information within the app
2.Saved Study Data

Users may save notes, Important Questions (IMP), and Previous Year Papers (PYP) for quick access. This saved study content is associated with the user account or device and is used only to improve the study experience.

2a.Stats and Local Storage

Study stats including your daily streak, best streak, exam countdown date, chapters saved, and days active are stored entirely on your device using browser local storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is never sent to our servers
  • Is not linked to your account or identity
  • Will be deleted if you clear your browser data or uninstall the app
3.How Information Is Used

Collected information may be used to:

  • Provide and maintain the app
  • Improve features and performance
  • Allow users to save and access study materials
  • Understand how users interact with the app
4.Data Protection

Notebook takes reasonable steps to protect user information. However, no online service can guarantee complete security.

5.Third-Party Services

If the app uses services such as Google login, those services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

6.Changes to Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the app develops. Updates will be reflected inside the app.

7.Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at lumo.feedback@gmail.com.

8.Draft Content Notice

Some Class 9 content in this app may be based on draft NCERT 2026–27 materials. Final chapter lists may vary from printed textbooks.
