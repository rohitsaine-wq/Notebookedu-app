About Notebook
Notebook
Study Smart. Score Better.
Version 1.0.0 · CBSE Class 9 & 10

Built for students, by someone who understands the exam pressure.

Notebook is a free study app for Class 9 and Class 10 CBSE students. It brings together chapter notes, important questions, and previous year papers — all in one clean, simple app that works even on slow internet.

Our goal is simple: help every student study smarter, revise faster, and feel more confident before exams — without needing expensive coaching or heavy study material.

📘
What We Cover
  • All 6 CBSE subjects — Science, Maths, English, Hindi, SST, Sanskrit
  • Class 9 · All subjects updated to new NCERT syllabus (2026–27)
  • Class 10 · All subjects as per NCERT syllabus (2026–27)
  • Chapter Notes · Important Questions · Previous Year Papers
  • Save chapters to your personal Library
  • Search across all subjects and chapters
  • Syllabus viewer — see all chapters at a glance before you start

✅ Note: Class 9 chapters are based on the confirmed CBSE 2026–27 syllabus, including new integrated textbooks (Kaveri for English, Ganga for Hindi, Ganita Manjari for Maths). Cross-check with your school-issued book if needed.

📊
My Stats

Notebook tracks your study habits to help you stay motivated and consistent.

  • Daily Streak — tracks how many consecutive days you have studied
  • Best Streak — your personal record, saved across sessions
  • Exam Countdown — set your board or exam date, see days remaining
  • Chapters Saved — total chapters bookmarked to your Library
  • Days Active — total days you have used the app
  • Progress by Subject — visual progress bar showing chapters saved per subject

All stats are stored locally on your device and are never shared with anyone.
